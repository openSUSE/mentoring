## Ready to Mentor? Here's How

<span>{% include icons/globe.svg %} [Contact us](https://github.com/openSUSE/mentoring#adding-new-project)
to get your project listed on our site

<span>{% include icons/pencil.svg %} Add issues for your project to our
[repository](https://github.com/openSUSE/mentoring/issues) using the issue template

<span>{% include icons/flag.svg %} Label your issues with your project's
name and assign yourself as the mentor

<span>{% include icons/chat.svg %} When contributors reach out to you
in the issue, help them get started
